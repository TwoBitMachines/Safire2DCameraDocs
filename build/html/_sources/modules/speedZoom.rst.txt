Speed Zoom
++++++++++

The camera will zoom based on the target's speed. Add as many threshold
speeds as necessary. Arrange them from the lowest threshold to the highest threshold.

.. list-table::
   :widths: 25 100
   :header-rows: 1

   * - Property
     - 

   * - Threshold
     - The speed the target must exceed or equal to trigger a zoom.

   * - Zoom
     - The camera zoom level.

   * - Speed
     - How quickly the camera should zoom.

   * - Axis
     - The axis in which to detect the target's speed.
